How to use the tools

Unit Price Calculator — how to use it

Standing in a store wondering whether the 500 g detergent or the 1.2 kg one is the better deal? Enter each product's price and size, and the calculator converts both to a price per 100 g so you can compare instantly. For bundles like "buy two, get one free", enter the number of items in the bundle and it computes the true price for the total volume. No sign-up, no install — and your numbers never leave your browser.

Character Counter — how to use it

Writing against a character limit — a cover letter, an essay, a social post, or a meta description? Paste your text and you instantly see counts with and without spaces, plus how much room is left against your target. It also shows byte counts (UTF-8 and EUC-KR) for systems that limit by bytes rather than characters. Everything is calculated inside your browser; nothing is sent to a server, so sensitive text is safe to paste.

Frequently asked questions

What is Toolhub?

Toolhub is a collection of free online tools — like the unit price calculator — for everyday tasks. There is nothing to install and no account to create: open your browser and it just works. Each tool is built to solve one problem, fast.

Are all the tools free?

Yes. Every tool listed on Toolhub is completely free — no payments, no subscriptions. If a paid feature ever appears, it will be clearly labeled on the tool's page.

Is my data sent to a server?

No. Toolhub tools are pure static pages: your input is processed entirely inside your browser and never transmitted. Convenience settings such as theme or language are stored only in this browser's localStorage.

How often are new tools added?

We ship new tools at a pace of two to three per week, starting from the most useful ideas. New tools appear on this page as soon as they launch, so check back from time to time.
