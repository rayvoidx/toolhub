Effective date: 2026-07-24

1. Information we collect

Toolhub (the "Service") can be used without creating an account, and we do not store the data you enter into any tool on a server. Whatever you type into a calculator, converter, or other tool is processed entirely inside your browser and is never transmitted to us or anyone else. Convenience preferences such as your chosen theme and language are stored only in your browser's localStorage on your own device, and you can clear them at any time through your browser settings. Separately, our advertising and measurement partners may collect limited technical data through cookies as described in Section 2.

2. Cookies and advertising

This Service is free and is supported by advertising. We use Google AdSense to display ads and Google Tag Manager to load Google's advertising and measurement tags. Google and its ad partners may use third-party cookies and similar technologies to serve and measure ads, including ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. These cookies are set by Google, not by us, and we do not have access to the personal data they may contain.

We implement Google's Consent Mode. For visitors in the European Economic Area (EEA), the United Kingdom, and Switzerland, advertising and analytics storage is denied by default until you make a choice, so no advertising cookies are set unless and until you consent. Where personalized advertising is not consented to, Google serves non-personalized ads and redacts ad identifiers.

You can control or opt out of personalized advertising at any time:

  • Manage your Google ad settings at Google Ads Settings.
  • Opt out of personalized ads from participating vendors at www.aboutads.info/choices.
  • Read how Google uses data from sites that use its services at How Google uses data when you use partner sites or apps.

3. Analytics

To understand how the Service is used and improve it, we may use anonymized traffic measurement provided through Google (loaded via Google Tag Manager). This measurement is subject to the same Consent Mode defaults described above and does not identify you personally.
