Contact

Effective date: 2026-07-24

Toolhub is run by an independent developer, and messages go straight to a real person — there is no support ticket queue in between. Whether something is broken, a result looks wrong, or you just have a question about how a tool works, you are welcome to reach out.

Email

The most direct way to reach us is by email at jhighllight@gmail.com. If you are reporting a bug, it helps to mention which tool you were using, what you entered, what you expected, and what happened instead — and whether you were on a phone or a computer. The more detail you include, the faster it can be fixed.

GitHub

The project lives on GitHub at github.com/rayvoidx, maintained by rayv. If you are comfortable with GitHub, that is a great place to file a detailed issue or follow what is being worked on.

Bug reports and tool suggestions welcome

Suggestions genuinely shape the roadmap. If there is a calculator or converter you keep wishing existed, tell us — a good chunk of the tools on the hub started as a request from someone who needed them. Bug reports are just as valued: a small, clear report about an edge case often leads to a quick fix that helps everyone.

What to expect

This is a solo, part-time project, so replies are not instant — but we do read every message and usually respond within a few days. Thanks for taking the time to get in touch.
