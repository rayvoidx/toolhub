About this length converter

This length converter turns a single number into every common distance unit at once — millimeters, centimeters, meters and kilometers on the metric side, plus inches, feet, yards and miles on the imperial side. It is handy when a product listing, a running route, a piece of furniture or a set of building plans mixes systems and you need the equivalent without hunting for a formula.

How to use it

Type your number in the Value box, then choose what it currently is under From and the unit you want under To. The big equivalent updates as you type, and the table below lists all eight units together so you can compare at a glance. Tap the swap arrow to reverse the pair, or press Copy to grab the headline result. Your last unit pair is remembered for next time.

How the conversion works

Every unit is defined against the meter, so the tool converts your value to meters first, then out to the target unit. The imperial side hangs off one exact fact: 1 inch = 2.54 cm, which makes 1 foot = 0.3048 m, 1 yard = 0.9144 m and 1 mile = 1609.344 m. For a worked example, take a height of 5 ft 9 in: that is 69 inches, and 69 × 2.54 = 175.26 cm, or about 1.75 m.

Tips & good to know

For rough mental math, 1 inch is about 2.5 cm, a meter is a little over 3 feet, and 8 km is roughly 5 miles. Remember that centimeters and inches suit everyday objects while kilometers and miles suit travel distances — mixing scales is the usual source of error. Because the factor 2.54 is exact, converting there and back returns your original number with no drift.

How many centimeters are in an inch?

Exactly 2.54. One inch equals 2.54 centimeters — an exact value fixed by the 1959 international yard and pound agreement, not a rounded approximation. So 10 in = 25.4 cm and 12 in (1 foot) = 30.48 cm. To convert cm to inches, divide the centimeters by 2.54.

What are the feet, yards and miles to meters conversion factors?

1 foot = 0.3048 m, 1 yard = 0.9144 m, and 1 mile = 1609.344 m (1.609344 km). These all follow from the inch: a foot is 12 inches, a yard is 3 feet, and a mile is 1760 yards. This converter uses these exact factors, so 5 miles = 8.04672 km and 100 m ≈ 328.08 ft.

How precise are the results?

Every conversion keeps up to 10 significant figures, then trailing zeros are trimmed for readability. Very large or very small numbers (roughly 1e15 and above, or below 1e-9) switch to scientific notation, so nothing is silently rounded away to zero. All values come from the exact metric definition of each unit.

Is my input sent to a server?

No. This length converter runs entirely in your browser — the number you type is never uploaded anywhere. The only thing saved is your last chosen unit pair (for example inches → centimeters), kept in your browser's local storage so it is ready next time. The value itself is not stored.
