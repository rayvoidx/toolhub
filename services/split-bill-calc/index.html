What if the bill doesn't split evenly?

Pick a round-up amount — nearest 1, 10, 100, or 1,000 — and this split bill calculator rounds every share up to a clean number. The little bit extra that gets collected shows up as the rounding surplus, which the organizer usually keeps or covers. Leave it on "Exact" if you'd rather split to the cent.

Does it include tip and tax?

Tip is optional: tap a preset (10 / 15 / 18 / 20%) or type your own, and it's added before the bill splitter divides. For tax, fold it into the total amount (or into the tip field), then split — one even share per person.

Even split (n빵) or itemized Dutch pay?

This tool does one thing fast: an even split where everyone pays the same share — exactly what people mean by n빵 계산기 or 회식비 정산. Itemized or weighted Dutch pay, where each person owes a different amount, is out of scope by design.

Is my amount sent to a server?

No. Every calculation runs locally in your browser. Nothing you type is uploaded, saved, or shared — this 더치페이 계산기 has no account, no tracking, and works offline.

How do I use the copy button?

Tap "Copy message" to grab a ready-to-send line like "Each of 4 pays 21,200 (total 84,800)" and paste it straight into your group chat. You can also tap any single figure — each share, total, or surplus — to copy just that number.
