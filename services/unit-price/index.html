Frequently asked questions

What is unit price (price per 100g)?

Unit price is a product's total price divided by its total amount — the price per gram, per milliliter or per item. This unit price calculator converts weight and volume products to price per 100g and price per 100ml so they are easy to compare at a glance. Even two products with different sizes can be compared directly once their unit prices line up.

Can it compare kg and g mixed together?

Yes. If one product is in kg and the other in g, the calculator automatically converts both to grams, so the price per unit is accurate. Volume works the same way — L and ml are normalized to ml. Just note that different types of units — weight (g), volume (ml) and count (item) — measure different things and cannot be compared, so enter units of the same type on both sides.

How do I enter a multipack (2+1 etc.)?

Enter the number of items in the items-per-pack field. For example, if a 500g product is sold as a pack of 3 for 9,000, enter amount 500, unit g and items per pack 3. The unit price is calculated from the total amount (amount × items per pack) to tell you which is cheaper.

Is my data sent to a server?

No. The price, amount and other inputs are never sent to a server — everything is calculated inside your browser. Only your last inputs are saved in this browser's localStorage so you can pick up where you left off; nothing is transmitted anywhere else.
